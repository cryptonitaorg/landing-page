Cryptonita

Crypto Copy Trading

A Startup focused on the world of cryptocurrencies

The world that is emerging after the growth of new technologies such as Blockchain and Artificial Intelligence requires an innovative and secure way to invest. Cryptonita's mission is to build an investment platform to match this new world.

We connect experts with lay people and intermediaries from all over the world, allowing them to interact and profit together.

Why?

Motivação

After entering the world of cryptocurrencies in the historic high of 2017 and losing almost everything for not understanding anything of the market and simultaneously seeing other people multiplying their capital exponentially, we decided that something could be done that would benefit both parties.

Something that would connect the experts with the begginers. And so Cryptonita Crypto Copy Trading was born.

História

Modelagem

Cryptonita began in a pre-acceleration program called Cotemig Startups, where a first version of the business was modeled. We later graduated from the Founder's Institute, one of the largest pre-acceleration programs in the world, where we created the current project idea. Today we are being accelerated and invested by WOW.
